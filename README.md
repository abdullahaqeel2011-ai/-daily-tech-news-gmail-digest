# 📰 Daily Tech News → Gmail Digest

Stay updated with the latest technology news effortlessly! This **n8n workflow** automatically fetches the top technology headlines using **NewsAPI** and sends a **beautifully formatted email digest** directly to your Gmail inbox. Perfect for professionals, businesses, and anyone who wants to save time while staying informed. ⚡

## ⚙️ Workflow Overview

This automation workflow consists of **4 simple steps**:

1. ⏰ **Schedule Trigger**  
   Runs automatically at your chosen time every day to trigger the workflow.

2. 🌐 **HTTP Request (News API)**  
   Fetches the latest top technology news headlines.

3. ✏️ **Edit Fields (Set Node)**  
   Extracts and formats the top 3 news headlines for the email digest.

4. 📧 **Gmail Node**  
   Sends a professionally formatted email digest with headlines to your inbox.

## 📧 Email Format

The emails sent by this workflow include:

- A friendly **good morning greeting** 🌅  
- **Justified paragraphs** with readable font sizes  
- **Top 3 technology headlines** in an ordered list 📰  
- A **footer** mentioning the workflow automation source

Example:

> **Good Morning!**  
> Here are today’s top technology news headlines:  
> 1️⃣ OpenAI launches new AI model  
> 2️⃣ Google announces AI updates  
> 3️⃣ Apple releases new product  
> Stay informed and keep exploring the world of technology. 🚀

## 🔗 How to Use

1. Open **n8n** and import the workflow JSON file.  

2. Configure your **NewsAPI API key** in the HTTP Request node.  

3. Connect your **Gmail account** in the Gmail node.  

4. Adjust the **Schedule Trigger** to your preferred time.  

5. Activate the workflow and enjoy daily tech news in your inbox automatically! 🚀
